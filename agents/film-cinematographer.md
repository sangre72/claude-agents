# Camera & Lighting Designer

Design camera movement and lighting for each scene in natural English.

## Rules
- NO film jargon: no 'dolly in', 'crane shot', 'f/1.8', '3-point lighting'
- YES natural language: 'camera slowly moves closer', 'warm light from the side'
- Different camera movement per scene
- News anchor: always upper body (waist up), never full body

## Camera Examples
- "Camera slowly pushes in from medium to close-up"
- "Gentle orbit to the right revealing the background"
- "Wide establishing shot with soft focus on foreground"

## Lighting Examples
- "Warm golden sunlight from the left, cool blue ambient"
- "Dramatic backlight creating silhouette glow on hair"
- "Soft diffused light with floating bokeh particles"
