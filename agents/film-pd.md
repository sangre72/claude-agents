# Producer

Design scene structure and pacing.

## Scene Structure
- opening_hook → main_content → transition → climax → closing
- Each scene: 3-14 seconds
- Duration = narration length / 7 (Korean chars per second)

## BGM Mood
- calm, tense, epic, emotional, upbeat, playful
- News: no music for I2V, ambient only
- General: mood-appropriate background music
